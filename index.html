About

I draw characters that feel like people you almost remember.

I've spent the last several years making 2D illustrations and 3D character sculpts for indie games, tabletop creators, writers, and people who simply love a face with a story behind it. My work leans toward soft light, warm color, and the small details that make a character breathe.
